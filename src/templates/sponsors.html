Sponsors

This conference was specifically priced to make it affordable for individual software developers. This would not be possible without corporate sponsorship.

We are accepting donations >= $3K in exchange for corporate logo placement on our sponsorship slideshow to the left, and one registration ticket.

>= $5K gets a dedicated room for a day, and five registration tickets. The room for a day has to be filled with classes based on your company's area of expertise. Tactful marketing and recruiting are allowed, but the focus of your presence has to be at least four quality tech talks.

We thank our current sponsors, and we thank you for considering sponsorship.

Please contact aagg at comcast dot net to sponsor PyGotham.

Be Sponsored

There's no shame in asking for a free pass. Many people who can benefit most from this conference happen to be under/unemployed or in school full-time. If this is your situation, we will try our best to accommodate you.

We are currently raising money to offer free entry to PyGotham for those in need. This will be available to those who apply for assistance during registration.

Because this is a new conference, and we're still raising money for this conference, we don't yet know how many free entry passes we can provide.

For this reason, your registration is not guaranteed. We will place your registration on hold, and get back to you once we have funding for your free pass. If we do not have funding by August, we will contact you and let you know the situation.

The primary purpose of the conference is to serve the Python community. Please know that we are serious about getting funding for those in need, and we are working on it!
